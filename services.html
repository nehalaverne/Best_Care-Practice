Services We Offer


Health Checks

We provide comprehensive and regular health checks. Such continuous care gives an oppportunity to our doctors to pick early warning signs of diseases. The Health Checks are booked under 45-49 year old Full Checks and over 75 Health Assessment bookings.

Immunisations

Immunisations help protect individuals against harmful and deadly diseases. Immunisations also prevent spread of such diseases in the community. Along with adult immunisations we also run and support the National Immunisation Program which includes vaccinating children over a course of period for total of 16 diseases.

Cosmetic Medicine

Cosmetic medicine can assist you to enhance your features and help you acheive the look that you have dreamed of. All you need to do is get in touch with our trained and experienced doctors, who will then go through your medical history with you. This will also be an opportunity for you to share your expectations with your doctor and know the expected outcome.

Skin Cancer Screening

It is recommended to have full skin skin checks once a year. Our doctors specialise in skin cancer screening and use advanced dermatoscopes to spot early signs of changes in the skin. The doctors can also help in excising skin cancers by performing biopsy.

In addition to the below listed services we provide routine care to the patients who are after general check-ups, scripts and referrals. We also provide emergency care and have trained nurses to patient triage and help the doctors with emergency care. We also perform Iron Infusions for patients who are referred by a specialist or another doctor. Pre-booking is essential to get Iron Infusion done.
